More About Growth Mindset

  • Demonstrates willingness to accept new, challenging concepts as an opportunity to learn and grow

  • Participates throughout sessions, engages in synchronous and asynchronous discussions and activities despite potential mistakes or incorrect answers

  • Provides and accepts feedback from peers, instructors and/or mentors, demonstrating that strong feedback can help all participants grow

  • Accepts the challenge of utilizng new technology tools and commits to mastering the use of these tools over time

  • Growth Mindset is important because it allows for individuals to grow and tackle new problems that would have been beyond their previous capabilities.

    A person with a growth mindset views any situation as an opportunity for growth. Throughout our journeys as life-long learners, there will certainly be challenges. Ultimately, whether we rise to the occasion or accept defeat will depend largely on our mindsets. The difference between "I don't know and "I don't know yet" is just a three letter word, but it's one that determines the sort of people we are.
